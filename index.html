About

Timothy Pallett is a conductor and choral clinician from Pretoria, South Africa. Having obtained his BMus from the University of Pretoria (cum laude) in 2020, he continued his studies to earn a Postgraduate Certificate in Education from Stadio Higher Education in 2021, an MMus specialising in Choral Conducting (cum laude) in 2024 and a Graduate Certificate in Choral Studies (cum laude) in 2025 from Missouri State University. During his time at Missouri State University he was awarded a Graduate Assistantship in both the Choral Studies Department and the Office of Global Education and Engagement where he helped facilitate the Memorandum of Understanding between the University of Pretoria and Missouri State University. Timothy has directed several High School choirs in the Gauteng area and has worked as a clinician with many more both domestically and abroad.
